About Me

I was born and raised in Latvia, a land of forests, lakes, and quiet beauty. My path has since taken me across the world — and today I live in Thailand, where colors, scents, and rhythms of life weave into my art. I am a self-taught artist, creating in mixed media: collage and textured painting. My works grow from fragments of stories — pre-loved fabrics reborn into something new, carrying traces of their past lives. Each piece is both personal and universal: a reminder of the harmony between creativity and sustainability. My inspiration comes from many places: the stillness of nature, the movement of dance, the depth of music, the wisdom of books, and the freedom of travel. Through art, I try to capture this dialogue — between the outer world and my inner one.
